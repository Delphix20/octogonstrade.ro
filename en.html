Profile

Integrated execution for infrastructure, utilities and site development works.

Octogon Strade Construct brings together teams, equipment and resources for coordinated site operations: roads, platforms, technical networks, exterior works, markings, enclosures, safety systems and seasonal services.

Services

Execution areas for infrastructure, utilities and site development works.

Public and private roads

Construction, rehabilitation and repair works for roads, access routes and traffic areas. We work on public and private projects, with attention to site preparation, work sequencing and the integration of related works.

  • Works for public and private traffic
  • Ground preparation and base layers
  • Coordinated execution with related works

Excavation and earth removal

Excavation, handling and earth removal works for site preparation. We operate for foundations, technical routes, platforms and areas that need to be brought to level or cleared quickly.

  • Excavations for foundations and utilities
  • Earth removal and transport
  • Preparation for the next work stages

Roundabouts and platforms

Construction and development of roundabouts, concrete platforms and concrete slabs. We handle traffic, access and operating areas where geometry, resistance and finish must be properly coordinated.

  • Development of traffic areas
  • Platforms for access, parking and operations
  • Concrete slab casting

Sewerage and undercrossings

Execution of sewerage, trenches and undercrossings integrated into infrastructure works. We plan routes so that utilities, drainage and road works function together.

  • Sewerage networks
  • Undercrossings for roads and platforms
  • Trenches and drainage works

Water networks

Works for water infrastructure and utility connections. We execute technical routes, connections and interventions compatible with road, platform or exterior development works.

  • Water supply networks
  • Undercrossings and technical routes
  • Integration with site routes

Green areas and parks

Development and maintenance of green areas, parks and exterior spaces for public or private use. We combine ground works with finishes, pedestrian areas and seasonal maintenance.

  • Green area development
  • Maintenance of planted areas
  • Park and pedestrian area development

Aggregates and materials

Supply of aggregates and materials for infrastructure and site development works. We provide suitable materials for ground preparation, backfilling, base layers and exterior works.

  • Crushed stone
  • Topsoil
  • Site aggregates

Signage, markings and access

Solutions for traffic organization, delimitation, access control and safety in operational areas. We execute road markings, pedestrian crossings, barriers and automated systems for clear flows.

  • Road markings and pedestrian crossings
  • Barriers and automated systems
  • Road and construction-site signage

Interior construction

Fit-out, renovation and finishing works for industrial, commercial or residential spaces. We cover demolition, partitioning, technical flooring and finishes adapted to the intended use of the space.

  • Demolition and partitioning
  • Epoxy, polyurethane, tile, marble and parquet flooring
  • Plasterboard walls and concrete slabs

Fences, railings and enclosures

Works for exterior boundaries, railings and custom-made metal elements. We execute solutions for protection, access, delimitation and completion of built areas.

  • Fences, railings and enclosures
  • Custom metal elements
  • Repairs and additions for existing boundaries

Fire safety and alarm systems

Technical systems for safety, monitoring and operational control. We integrate fire safety, alarm, video surveillance and signaling solutions for buildings and operational areas.

  • Fire safety and alarm systems
  • Video surveillance
  • Control and signaling systems

Snow removal

Snow removal services and seasonal interventions during winter. We provide support for private roads, platforms, access routes and areas where operational continuity matters.

  • Interventions for access and circulation
  • Seasonal operational services
  • Support for platforms and private roads
